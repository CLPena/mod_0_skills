Class: Waitstaff

Attributes:
firstName ["Mike", "Andy", "Maggie", "Ellen"] (array)
lastName ["Hart", "Jimenez", "Smith", "Jones"] (array)
address (string)
hourlyWage (float)
hoursPerWeek (float)
workingToday (boolean)
nickname (string)
pronoun (string)
birthDate (dateTime)
yearsEmployed (integer)
shirtSize (string)
pantSize (string)


Methods:
calculatePaycheck (multiplies the hourlyWage float by the hoursPerWeek float)
printNameTag (prints strings from nickname and pronoun attributes)
printPayStub (prints firstName, lastName, address, hourlyWage, and hoursPerWeek)
orderUniform (uses strings from shirtSize and pantSize to fill out order form)
coverShift (uses workingToday boolean and firstName and lastName strings to print a list of available waitstaff to cover a shift today)
